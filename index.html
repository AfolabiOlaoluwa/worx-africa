Subject: An Overview of WORX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED: Our Core Operations and Innovative Products

To Whom It May Concern,

This serves to provide a comprehensive overview of the diverse business activities and strategic direction of WORX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED. As stipulated in our memorandum, our company is established with a multi-faceted objective to operate and create value across several key sectors of the economy. Our core business activities are broad, demonstrating our capacity for significant and varied enterprise. These activities include:

  • Technology and E-commerce: The design, development, operation, and maintenance of web technologies, internet portals, e-commerce platforms, and a full suite of e-business solutions.
  • General Trade and Commerce: The business of general trading, including the import, export, sale, and distribution of a wide array of commodities and goods, supported by warehousing and processing capabilities.
  • Energy and Industrial Services: The supply, marketing, and distribution of oil and gas products, including pure petroleum, industrial chemicals, and bitumen, as well as operating oil supply base services and petrol filling stations.
  • Agriculture and Agribusiness: Engagement in agricultural farming, food processing, the production of fertilizers, and the comprehensive management of the farm-to-market supply chain, including producing, preserving, and marketing farm products.
  • Real Estate and Property Development: The acquisition, development, sale, and management of land, buildings, and real estate properties, acting as agents, developers, and general contractors.
  • General Merchandise and Industrial Supply: The global trade of manufactured goods and raw materials, serving as general merchants and industrialist distributors.

Building upon our foundational strength in technology, we are exceptionally proud to channel our expertise into creating innovative software products that address specific market needs. These products represent the future of our company and our commitment to leveraging technology for practical solutions. Our recently launched flagship products are:

  • Cartlinq by Worx: This is a powerful e-commerce software solution designed to empower merchants. It enables them to sell their products seamlessly both offline and online, integrating directly with numerous social media platforms. A key feature is its robust payment system, which allows businesses to accept payments and share direct payment links with customers, simplifying transactions and boosting sales.
  • Cooperative by Worx: A revolutionary cooperative savings society, uniquely distributed and managed via WhatsApp for ultimate accessibility. It introduces a groundbreaking "eat-your-cake-and-have-it" financial policy that is one of its kind. Members are able to spend from their savings and contributions while continuing to earn full dividends as if the funds were never withdrawn. This novel approach provides unparalleled financial flexibility and value in the finance and savings space.

Our diverse business interests provide us with a stable foundation and unique insight into various industries, which in turn fuels our technological innovations. We are confident that this synergistic approach will continue to drive our growth and establish WORX TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED as a leader in both traditional and digital markets.

We look forward to forging a path of continued innovation and success

Olaoluwa Afolabi
